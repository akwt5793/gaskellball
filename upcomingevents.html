The October Gaskell Ball: Saturday, October 24th, 2015

Gaskells is back for a spooktacular event! Our next ball is the October Gaskell Ball, our largest and most spectacular event of the year. While you can come in formal attire, Halloween is a chance to shine, so start finding/making your costume now. And bring your camera, as there we be all sorts of interesting costumes to be seen.

Costumes can include historical costumes, modern formal wear, fictional characters, cosplay, steampunk and more. Be elegant Be dramatic. Be outrageous! Please check our 'What to Wear' page for more information

Doors open at 7:30, dancing begins at 8:30.

Admission:

$20 in advance.

$25 at the door.

There is an afternoon dance class from 3:30pm - 6:00pm. (Plenty of time to change into costume afterwards). Don't forget to bring danceable shoes to the class! $10
